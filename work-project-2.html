Skuot - Space for work

Branding and Website Case Study

Overview

Skuot is a new coworking space in central Warsaw. They needed a new brand and website to attract creative people looking for a relaxed place to work together. My role involved creating a brand that visually communicated this dynamic and welcoming atmosphere.

Branding Elements

Logo Design

The logo concept was inspired by the core ideas of networking, communication, and the exchange of inspiration, visually represented through elements reminiscent of Wi-Fi signals. This symbolizes an open community and the power of collaborative work.

Color Palette

To establish a modern and energetic feel, I selected a contrasting color scheme of vibrant yellow and bold black. This palette aims to resonate with creative and innovative professionals.

Illustrations

Injecting a sense of playfulness and approachability, I incorporated doodle-style illustrations throughout the brand. These elements contribute to a light and informal atmosphere within the coworking space.

Branding and Website Design for a Coworking Space

For Skuot, a Warsaw-based coworking space dedicated to creative professionals, I designed a comprehensive visual identity and an intuitive website. The branding – featuring a logo inspired by a Wi-Fi signal, an energetic color palette of yellow and black, and custom doodle-style illustrations – communicates openness, modernity, and a collaborative spirit. The website design, fully responsive and enriched with my own professional interior photography, attractively showcases Skuot's offerings and unique atmosphere. The result is a cohesive image of an inspiring space that effectively builds a strong community.

Website Design

Intuitive User Experience

The website was designed with a focus on intuitive navigation and a fully responsive layout, ensuring optimal viewing across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

Engaging Content Presentation

The website content was structured to present Skuot's offerings in an attractive and easily digestible format, emphasizing the unique benefits and atmosphere of the space.

Professional Photography

I also took professional photos of the interior to show the true vibe of Skuot. These shots are featured throughout the website to highlight the space and what it offers.

Final Design

A Strong Brand and Foundation for the Skuot Community

With a consistent and visually appealing brand identity and website, Skuot presents itself as a friendly and inspiring coworking space for creative professionals in Warsaw. Carefully developed design solutions have created a solid foundation for building a strong and engaged community.
