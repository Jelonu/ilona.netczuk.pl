Selected Visual Identity Projects

PyWaw Summit A Branding Challenge for a Tech Conference

The design challenge was to develop a powerful and appealing brand identity for PyWaw Summit, a Warsaw-based programming conference. The visual identity needed to convey the event's dynamic atmosphere, its high level of technical expertise, and its core association with the Python programming language. Leveraging the snake motif, instantly recognizable as a Python symbol, I crafted a dynamic logo form with contemporary typography. The logo is defined by its inherent simplicity and impactful expressiveness, ensuring effortless brand recognition and lasting memorability within the developer community.

Ortotech - Dental Technology Lab Branding

Visual identity design for Ortotech, a specialist in advanced dental technology. To underscore the company's core values of professionalism, precision, and meticulous attention to detail, a unified brand image was developed. This was achieved through a refined color palette of blues and grays, evoking trust, professionalism, and a contemporary feel. A central feature of the Ortotech dental technology lab logo project is a logomark incorporating the Schwarz plate – a symbol instantly recognizable within the field of orthodontics. The overarching objective was to establish a professional and consistent brand presence that accurately reflects the company's precision and modern approach.

Aesthetic Medicine Clinic Branding

The design challenge was to create an elegant and sophisticated brand identity for an aesthetic medicine clinic, visually embodying its character and values. The logotype is inspired by the letter "U" (for "Uroda," meaning Beauty) and gentle floral motifs, representing both beauty and naturalness. A subtle cross, interwoven into the design, subtly conveys professionalism and medical expertise. The project aimed to establish a brand image that resonates with femininity, youthfulness, harmony, and a focus on natural beauty.

Theater Poster Design

Designed a poster for the play (MASKA)RADA GMINY, a satirical commentary on contemporary reality that delves into the problem of social divisions. This work serves as an artistic rebellion against these divisions, offering a humorous yet sharp satire on the current state of affairs. The play probes the underlying motives behind stated ideologies and questions how our society has become so deeply polarized.
