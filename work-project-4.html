LOG GE

Intelligent Asset Management Software

Overview

LOG GE is an advanced asset management platform designed for enterprise clients managing complex infrastructure and equipment portfolios. The system provides real-time tracking, maintenance scheduling, and predictive analytics.

This project involved redesigning the entire user experience to make complex data more accessible and actionable for facility managers and operations teams.

The Challenge

The existing system was data-rich but difficult to navigate. Users struggled to find critical information quickly, and the interface didn't scale well for organizations managing thousands of assets across multiple locations.

Dashboard Design

Design Approach

We implemented a dashboard-first approach with customizable widgets, allowing users to prioritize the information most relevant to their role. Advanced filtering and search capabilities make it easy to drill down into specific assets or locations.

Key Improvements

  • • Unified dashboard with real-time status updates
  • • Visual asset hierarchy and location mapping
  • • Intelligent maintenance alerts and scheduling
  • • Mobile-responsive design for field teams
  • • Advanced reporting and analytics tools
