Nussknacker

Color Pallete Optimization

Overview

Nussknacker is an advanced yet user-friendly low-code tool that empowers businesses to intelligently process real-time data. This enables them to react faster to changes, automate processes, and make better business decisions.

Visit website

Learn more at https://nussknacker.io →

Step 1 – Discovery phase

Based on user feedback, we've enhanced our app to better meet your needs. Now, you can conveniently organize Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) from different teams by pinning them in a dedicated section. We've also improved the target-setting feature, allowing you to set goals on a monthly, quarterly, or yearly basis.

User Feedback

Problem statement

Perdoo lacks centralized KPI tracking for different teams, hindering goal monitoring and alignment. Limited target-setting functionality restricts users' ability to define varied timeframes. User feedback highlights the need for improvements to enhance functionality and flexibility in goal management.

Step 2 – Ideation & MVP

During the initial concept phase, we explored multiple versions to address the identified problem statement. We developed various mock-ups to visualize the list of Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) in conjunction with the corresponding months and targets.

Low Fidelity Wireframes

Through careful consideration and user feedback, we have determined the optimal structure for this feature and defined the Minimum Viable Product (MVP) accordingly. We decided which features to work on first using a RICE prioritization method.

Step 3 – Prototyping

Using Figma, I developed a working prototype for user testing on volunteered participants. The high-fidelity version incorporates existing components and introduces new ones as necessary.

Prototype

Step 4 – Validation

We conducted a series of user tests on the prototype, gathering valuable feedback to improve its usability. Based on the feedback received, we made necessary changes to achieve the most user-friendly and effective result.

In addition, we conducted internal design review meetings to gather technical and UX input from our team members. These collaborative sessions allowed us to leverage their expertise and insights to further enhance the design of our solution.

Final effect

From day one we've received a lot of positive feedback regarding the new way of organizing company KPIs as well as a new way of setting targets. We also drafted future iterations of the solution that potentially would improve usability and make it even more powerful.
